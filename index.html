About HandyPro

Founded in 2004, HandyPro was born from a simple vision: to revolutionize the home repair industry by combining exceptional craftsmanship with uncompromising reliability. Our founder, a third-generation tradesman, recognized a critical gap in the market—homeowners needed a service provider they could trust completely. Starting with a single truck and a commitment to excellence, we've grown into the region's most trusted handyman service, built entirely on word-of-mouth recommendations and a dedication to doing things right the first time.

Today, HandyPro stands as a beacon of integrity in home services. Our mission is simple yet powerful: to deliver stress-free home solutions through expert craftsmanship, transparent communication, and unwavering reliability. We believe every homeowner deserves a handyman they can trust—someone who shows up on time, completes work on budget, and stands behind every project with genuine pride. Our core values of honesty, excellence, and customer-first service guide every decision we make, ensuring that when you call HandyPro, you're not just hiring a service—you're partnering with a team that genuinely cares about your home and your peace of mind.
